==========
References
==========

For a better understanding of OSCAR/requirements, its management and the
role of the PoC it is recommended to study several background
references, apart from the information made available on the various
OSCAR websites. Three types of reference material is available:

1. Legal documentation and informative or documents published by WMO or
   INFCOM

2. Documents and presentations provided during the various workshops,
   organized for PoCs.

3. Historic material; may not up-to-date anymore but valable to
   understand the development and present structure of
   OSCAR/Requirements
